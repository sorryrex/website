About Us

Founded on strong values and a passion for education, PhilTech provides a nurturing environment for students to thrive both academically and socially.

Our Mission

Philippine Technological Institute of Science, Arts and Trade, Inc. (PHILTECH) is dedicated to give quality education to the development of every Filipino. It aims to hone students who are values oriented and physically, academically, socially and spiritually committed to the achievement of life-long learning and service to the nation.

Our Vision

PHILTECH shall emerge as the only technical school in the Philippines that can provide academic excellence, appropriate technology and produce graduates who are globally competitive and shall serve as pillar in this changing world.

Admissions

Join our vibrant school community. Here’s what you need to know:

  • Our application period runs from January to June each year.
  • Eligible students are evaluated based on academic records, recommendations, and entrance assessments.
  • Financial aid and scholarship opportunities are available to qualified applicants.
  • We encourage prospective families to attend our open house events and campus tours.
  • Apply online or contact the admissions office for detailed information.

Academics

PhilTech offers specialized tracks for Senior High School and College to shape your future in technology and beyond.

Senior High School Strands

  • HUMMS: Humanities and Social Sciences
  • ICT: Information and Communications Technology
  • HE: Home Economics
  • ABM: Accountancy, Business, and Management
  • TVL: Technical-Vocational-Livelihood

College Courses

  • BSCS - Bachelor of Science in Computer Science
  • BTVTED - Bachelor of Technical-Vocational Teacher Education
  • BSOA - Bachelor of Science in Office Administration
